World Conflicts

It's normal for us to be caught up in our day to day lives and forget that in our world there are struggles, challenges, and conflicts happening to people.

This data visualization looks at conflicts outside of North America from May 1st, 2020 to now and shows the amount of fatalities from riots, attacks on civilians, battles, and other sources. The higher the bar, the more fatalities.

- Designed by Steven Shoemaker Source: ACLED(acleddata.com)
